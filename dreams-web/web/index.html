About German Articles

Including a large dictionary of german nouns, a quiz, some helpful grammar rules and articles, this application will help you to write gramatically correct texts and improve your knowledge of the language.

Write correct texts

German Articles contains more than 33 000 words and still grows. You can send missing words directly from within the app and you will find them in the next version.

Stay focused

You can fast and easy check a German Article on your mobile device while writing on your laptop. The app saves you from switching windows, searching for a word and open a bunch of websites.
